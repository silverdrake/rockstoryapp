GOLDEN RECORD
Bands that have at least one vertical ACTIVITY card played on them are eligible for the Golden Record award.
For each eligible band, check the number of vertical ACTIVITY cards and add their current score.
The band with the highest value earns this year's Golden Record award, which will be given to their label.
Tied bands win one award each, ex-aequo (even if they belong to the same label).
